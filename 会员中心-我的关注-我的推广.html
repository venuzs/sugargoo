Announcement on the delays in the package in the Canada-Toronto port affected by the epidemic prevention and control
Dear SUGARGOO user

latest news! According to the notice from China Post, due to the impact of the epidemic prevention and control at the Toronto Port in Canada, the local package processing center cannot guarantee normal operations. From now on, the packages that use EMSE-EMS/EUB routes (under China Post) to the port will be affected. The impact of serious delays, China Post is currently actively coordinating the handling plan, and other lines will not be affected temporarily.

SUGARGOO warmly reminds users to try their best to avoid the above delays or postpone delivery when they carry their bags!

For any logistics/related information, please contact the online customer service for consultation, SUGARGOO wishes you a happy shopping
